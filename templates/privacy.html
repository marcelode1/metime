Privacy Policy

How {{ app_name }} handles your information.

We collect the contact and health information you provide so your massage therapist can give you safe, personalized care.

Your name, contact details, and the health information you enter in your intake form (medical history, medications, allergies, areas of concern, and similar).

Your health information is private. It is used only by the office/therapist to provide and improve your care. We do not sell your information.

Only the office staff can view your health information. Other clients cannot see it.

Your choices

You can update your intake form at any time, or contact the office to request changes or deletion of your information.
